Behind the music
आत्मा की वाणी

SoulVani is a Hindi neo-soul project for slow listeners.

The songs are written longhand, in notebooks, in margins, on hotel stationery in cities the artist will probably never see again. They are composed at the piano late at night, recorded warm, mixed without urgency.

Three albums — Ishq Beqarar, Tu Hai Yahin, Adhure Lamhein — trace the same love through three weathers. Each is sung in Hindi and Urdu, with the diction of poetry, not pop.

The Collector's Edition is the only printed home for these songs. Everything else lives on streaming, but the booklet, the certificate, the print and the signature are how SoulVani says this was a real thing — it really happened.

— SoulVani, 2026
